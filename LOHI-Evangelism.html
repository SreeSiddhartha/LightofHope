LOHI - Evangelism

Evangelism

We have teams that go to various villages where they distribute pamphlets and show the Jesus movie and conduct evening gospel meetings. Many people are giving their hearts to Jesus and getting saved. We have also been conducting crusades in some towns. Thousands of people attend the crusades and the majority are saved and healed by these crusades. A small church is planted every few months through this ministry.
