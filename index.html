Who We Are?

Light of Hope International
faith, hope, love. --Corinthians 13 : 13

Light of Hope international is an indigenous organization started in 2000 in the state of Andhra Pradesh South India, working to bring souls to Christ. Light of Hope International is a multi-faceted ministry that reaches out to our communities with the love of God both spiritually and physically.

Our Director

God gave a vision to Velma and anointed her. She has a calling for the nations. God has fulfilled her passion for the lost souls by leading her to start the Light of Hope international an indigenous organization in August 2000 in Ongole, Andhra Pradesh, South India. Supporting the Old Project

Light of Hope International (LOHI) endeavors to meet the physical and spiritual needs of elderly people who have been disowned and abused by their families for one reason or another. Due to social norms, high illiteracy, lack of awareness about their rights, and more, many elderly women who live with their families are abused by those same families.
These conditions have forced some of the aged population, including both men and women, to live on the streets in deplorable conditions. LOHI exists to provide safe and secure housing, beds, toilets and food for such disadvantaged individuals.

How you can help:

  • 1. Sponsor one of these elderly people for $50 per month.
  • 2. Donate funds to build a home to care for 40+ adults ($31, 428 approximate cost).
  • 3. Contribute towards the creation of a vegetable garden and rice paddy that will provide long-term sustainability for the home ($25,000).
